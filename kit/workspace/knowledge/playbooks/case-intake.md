# Case Intake

Good cases are:

- reproducible
- scoped to one failure or opportunity
- connected to user or business impact
- backed by evidence

Required fields:

- case id
- symptom
- expected behavior
- observed behavior
- severity
- source
- evidence

Preferred extra fields:

- attempted approaches
- current blockers
- what would count as new evidence

Preferred severity meaning:

- `low`
  - useful but not urgent
- `medium`
  - should improve soon
- `high`
  - repeated or clearly user-harming
- `critical`
  - safety, trust, or severe business damage
